igpay atinlay, orway "igpayway atinlayway" isway away anguagelay amegay orway argotway inway ichwhay englishway ordsway areway alteredway, usuallyway by addingway away abricatedfay uffixsay orway by ovingmay ethay onsetway orway initialway onsonantcay orway onsonantcay usterclay ofway away ordway otay ethay endway ofway ethay ordway andway addingway away ocalicvay ablesyllay otay eatecray uchsay away uffixsay. orfay exampleway, "ikipediaway" ouldway ecomebay "ikipediawayway" (ethay "w" isway ovedmay omfray ethay eginningbay andway ashay "ayway" appendedway otay eatecray away uffixsay). ethay objectiveway isway otay oncealcay ethay ordsway omfray othersway otnay amiliarfay ithway ethay ulesray. ethay eferenceray otay atinlay isway away eliberateday isnomermay; igpay atinlay isway implysay away ormfay ofway argotway orway argonjay unrelatedway otay atinlay, andway ethay amenay isway usedway orfay itsway englishway onnotationscay asway away angestray andway oreignfay-oundingsay anguagelay. itway isway ostmay oftenway usedway by oungyay ildrenchay asway away unfay ayway otay onfusecay eoplepay unfamiliarway ithway igpay atinlay.
